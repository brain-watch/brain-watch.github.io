We're Brainwatch

Brainwatch is aiming to detect and record minor concussions.

We are using ultra low-power LoRaWAN to build a large-scale and long-distance IoT safety system.

Learn more

Long Range

Brainwatch system utilizes LoRaWan for low bandwidth, long range (up to 7 mi.), and low power communication.

Analyzable data

Utilizing IoT, data is immediately available on the Internet, and could be analyzed in real time, or stored individually.

Extendable Use Case

Brainwatch's system could be used not only for football, but also for skiing, ice skating, or ice hockey.
